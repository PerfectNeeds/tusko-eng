Gantries Specifications

The model TU400/26's capacity is 400 ton/hr.
We can manufacture gantries it’s capacity is up to 600 ton/hr as customer’s desire.
Current model can move longitudionally, and transversally relative to the boom, and it can pivot rotate in it’s location.
The gantry may be manufactured with a rotatable tower boom, or can use wheels to rotate the boom. The current model is provided with a boom that carries the suction pipes, bob cat hoist, the boom length is 24 meter and it can be extended to 28 meter as per customer’s desire.
Our standard gantry product will be 400 TPH, 25m lenght boom. Its specifcations, and drawings are attached.
Air filters: cylindrical filters that cleaned by compressing air inside it.
Movement: Electro-Hydraulic.
Steering: Electro-Hydraulic.
Crane can carry a bob cat weights from 2 ton to 10 ton.
Vertical and horizontal telescopic tubes, powered by electric hoists.
Electro-hydraulic control by a remote control with cable.
Double spout loading of 2 trucks at the same time to save loading time.

Type T-400/26m Specifications


THE GANTRY:
  • Gantry on eight rubber wheels, with steering and hydraulic traction by four hydraulic motors.
  • Travel speed 15 m /min.
  • Two pneumatically powered loading spouts.


THE CABIN:
  • Two main electric motors of 250 kW each, 400 Volts, 50 Hz (IP 55)
  • Two heavy duty roots blowers
  • Rotating airlock of 600 mm diameter with adjustable blade tips, replaceable rings and by‑pass for emptying filter;
  • Automatic self‑cleaning filter with its compressor– 22 kW
  • Hydraulic power group 30 kW.
  • Onboard I-Beam for lowering of motors and blowers.


THE BOOM:
  • 26 metres main boom with gangway for services. Telescopic actions by remote controls. 
  • Raise lower boom action with 22 KW powered main boom hoist.
  • Powered trolley for telescopic horizontal pipe, .
  • 3 ton Bob cat hoist.
  • Remote control for luffing hoist & powered telescoping pipe trolley.           

 

THE PIPING:

The suction pipe system supported by the boom includes the following elements:

  • Vertical suction nozzle dia. 350 mm;
  • Vertical telescopic tube in 2 parts thickness 4 mm in wear resistant steel with remotely controlled power chain block.
  • Flexible pipe made of wear resistant steel conical sections with, connecting the vertical telescopic to the travelling elbow.
  • Travelling suction elbow.
  • Horizontal telescopic tube made in 2 parts thickness 6 mm.
  • Flexible pipe between the filter hopper and the horizontal telescopic tube made of wear resistant steel.

THE PNEUMATIC AND HYDRAULIC MOVEMENTS:

The following movements are operated by a remote control box from ship's deck:

  • Gantry movement forward and backward.
  • Direct to pivot Steering.
  • Lifting and lowering jacks. 
  • Lifting and lowering of the boom.
  • Horizontal and vertical movements of the suction telescopic pipes.

A DIESEL GENERATOR AND AN ADDITIONAL FUEL TANK:
  • Diesel generator of 750 KVA.
  • Fuel tank of 5000 litres.

CAPACITY:
  • Maximum capacity, suction nozzle in free flowing grain (density 0.75): 400 TONS/HOUR.
