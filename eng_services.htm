Engineering services:

The design process of large cranes starts with recognition of the necessary boom length, and the loading on it. The method of movement and steering around the roads and obstacles is established. the main functions of the crane, and the auxiliary functions necessarily performed by the equipment is identified.
The structural analysis is performed using the latest analytical Finite Element analysis programs. The stresses are checked and displayed by colors, and colors by the program output. Large stress areas are corrected in materials and design shapes.
The structural drawings are reviewed according to international standards and approved before release.
