400 tph explosion proof Grain handling system for 50000 ton silo.

Installation of complete dust explosion proof 400 TPH material handling system for grains inside a 50,000 tons flat storage silo. System includes:
• Reception hopper, and feeder ready for 50 ton trailers, inside a rain shed.
• 400 TPH bucket elevator, 28 meters of height, with HDPE buckets, and dust explosion proof construction.
• 400 TPH conveyor belt, 106 meters length, with discharge tripper on top of flat silo.
• 28 meter access steel tower for support of bucket elevator.
